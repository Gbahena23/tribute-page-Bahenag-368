Phil Knight

Former CEO of Nike, inc

Phil Knight is Chairman Emeritus of NIKE, Inc. Mr. Knight is a co-founder of the company, leading Nike from a small partnership founded on a handshake to the world’s largest footwear, apparel and equipment company.

Here is a Timeline of Knight Life:

    1964 - Blue Ribbon Sports (BRS) Founded

  • Phil Knight and his former track coach, Bill Bowerman, founded Blue Ribbon Sports as the U.S. distributor for a Japanese shoe brand called Onitsuka Tiger.
  • This marks the beginning of Knight’s journey into the sportswear industry. BRS eventually became Nike.

    • 1971 - Creation of Nike and the "Swoosh"

  • Knight and Bowerman officially launched Nike, named after the Greek goddess of victory. The iconic Swoosh logo was designed by graphic designer Carolyn Davidson for just $35.
  • The Swoosh quickly became a globally recognized symbol, marking the start of Nike’s visual identity.

    • 1972 - First Nike Shoes Released

  • Nike launched its first line of shoes, featuring the innovative Waffle Trainer sole design by Bill Bowerman.

    • 1980 - Nike Goes Public

  • Offering its stock on the New York Stock Exchange.

    • 1984 - Air Jordan Partnership

  • Nike signed a groundbreaking endorsement deal with NBA superstar Michael Jordan, leading to the creation of the Air Jordan line.

    • 1996 - Global Expansion

  • Nike signed global athletes across various sports, including Tiger Woods (golf), Serena Williams (tennis), and Cristiano Ronaldo (soccer).

    • 2000 - Nike and Sustainability Initiatives

  • Nike started emphasizing sustainability in its production process, focusing on reducing waste and improving labor conditions worldwide.

    • 2003 - Launch of Nike+iPod

  • Nike partnered with Apple to launch the Nike+iPod Sports Kit, which allowed runners to track their performance data using an iPod.

    • 2006 - Nike Acquires Converse

  • Nike acquired the Converse brand, extending its influence beyond performance sportswear into casual and lifestyle footwear.

    • 2012 - Nike’s New Headquarters

  • Nike opened its new Northwest headquarters in Beaverton, Oregon, featuring state-of-the-art facilities and a focus on innovation.

    • 2015 - Phil Knight Retires as CEO

  • Phil Knight stepped down as CEO, with Mark Parker taking over. However, Knight remained as chairman and continued to influence Nike's direction.
